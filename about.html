About Essence Body Fragrance

Our story, mission, and values

Our Story

Essence Body Fragrance was born from a simple idea: that feeling good and smelling good should go hand in hand.

Founded in 2020 by fragrance enthusiasts Alex and Jordan, Essence Body Fragrance began as a small operation creating premium body sprays for active lifestyles. What started as a passion project quickly expanded as we perfected our craft and developed our signature scent profiles.

We noticed a gap in the market for high-quality fragrances that could keep up with an active lifestyle while offering sophisticated scent profiles that evolve throughout the day. Our sprays are designed to provide long-lasting fragrance that adapts to your body chemistry.

Today, Essence Body Fragrance continues to grow while staying true to our founding principles of quality, performance, and sustainability. Every spray we create is made with the same care and attention to detail that defined our very first batch.

Our Ingredients

We believe in transparency about what goes into our products. Our fragrances are crafted using premium ingredients that are safe for you and the environment.

Essential Oils

We use pure essential oils extracted from plants, flowers, and fruits to create our signature scents.

Free From Harmful Chemicals

Our products are free from parabens, phthalates, and other potentially harmful ingredients.

Sustainable Sourcing

We carefully select suppliers who share our commitment to ethical and sustainable practices.

Long-Lasting Formulas

Our unique formulations ensure your fragrance lasts throughout the day without frequent reapplication.
