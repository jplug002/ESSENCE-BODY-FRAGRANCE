Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Delivery within Accra typically takes 1-2 business days. For other regions in Ghana, delivery may take 2-5 business days depending on your location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, mobile money transfers, and bank transfers.

Can I return or exchange products?

Yes, we accept returns and exchanges within 7 days of delivery if the product is unused and in its original packaging. Please contact us to initiate a return or exchange.

Are your products tested on animals?

No, all our products are cruelty-free and have never been tested on animals.

How long does the fragrance last?

Our body sprays are formulated to last 6-8 hours on average, depending on your skin type and activity level. For maximum longevity, apply to pulse points on clean, moisturized skin.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we only ship within Ghana. We're working on expanding our shipping options to other countries in the future.

Shipping & Returns

Shipping Policy

We strive to deliver your products as quickly and efficiently as possible.

  • Orders are processed within 24 hours of placement.
  • Delivery within Accra: 1-2 business days.
  • Delivery to other regions in Ghana: 2-5 business days.
  • Shipping fees are calculated based on your location and will be displayed at checkout.
  • You will receive a call or text message when your order is out for delivery.

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not, we're happy to help with a return or exchange.

  • Returns and exchanges are accepted within 7 days of delivery.
  • Products must be unused and in their original packaging.
  • To initiate a return or exchange, please contact us via WhatsApp or email.
  • Refunds will be processed within 7 business days after we receive the returned item.
  • For hygiene reasons, body sprays can only be returned if sealed and unopened.
